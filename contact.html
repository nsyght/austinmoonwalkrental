Contact FAQ

Quick answers before you reach out.

How quickly do you respond to quote requests?
We respond to all quote requests same day - typically within a few hours during business hours (Mon-Sun 9AM-5PM). For urgent same-week bookings, call (512) 555-1212 directly for the fastest response.
Can I book online?
Yes - online booking is available 24/7 at jump-aroundpartyrentals.com/category/. You can browse the full inventory, check availability and reserve your unit at any hour. A deposit locks your date and unit.
Are your rentals insured in Texas?
Yes. All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, who is fully licensed and insured in Texas. Every unit carries a current Texas Amusement Ride Compliance Sticker and commercial liability coverage. See our About page for full details.
What cities do you deliver to?
We deliver across Austin and the full Greater Austin metro - Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Hutto, Leander, Round Rock, Liberty Hill, Killeen, Belton and Temple. Delivery fees vary by distance. Include your city in the quote request and we will confirm the fee.
Do you deliver to Austin parks?
Yes. Austin park rentals require a permit from Austin Parks and Recreation at (512) 974-6700. Secure your park permit before or alongside booking your rental. See our main FAQ for Austin park details.
What is your cancellation policy?
Weather cancellations are handled case-by-case. If severe weather (lightning, high winds) prevents safe setup we work with you to reschedule at no extra charge. Contact (512) 555-1212 for specific cancellation policy details on your booking.
