Who We Are

Austin's Moonwalk and Bounce House Rental Source

Austin Moonwalk Rental is your local source for moonwalk and inflatable rentals across Austin TX and the Greater Austin metro. All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, who is fully licensed and insured in Williamson County and Travis County, Texas.

Jump Around Party Rentals is based in Round Rock at 3616 Bass Loop and has been delivering bounce houses, moonwalks, water slides and obstacle courses across the Austin metro for years. Their fleet covers Austin, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Hutto, Leander, Round Rock, Liberty Hill, Killeen, Belton and Temple.

Every rental includes delivery, professional setup, a full safety inspection and complete teardown. Online booking is available 24/7 at jump-aroundpartyrentals.com/category/ and office hours run Monday through Sunday 9AM to 5PM.

Safety and Standards

Licensed, Insured and Texas-Compliant

All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, who is fully licensed and insured in Texas. Here is what that means for your Austin party.

🛡️

Insured Operator

All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, who is fully licensed and insured in Williamson County and Travis County, Texas. Every unit carries a current Texas Amusement Ride Compliance Sticker.

🔍

Pre-Event Inspection

Every bounce house, water slide and combo unit is safety inspected before each rental. Our team checks anchoring, blower operation, seams and entry points before the first child bounces.

🧼

Sanitized Between Rentals

Every unit is fully cleaned and sanitized after each use. Your kids jump on equipment prepared specifically for your event - not rolled straight from the previous party.

🌬️

Weather Policy

We monitor Austin weather closely. Units are shut down in winds over 15-20 mph and never operated during lightning. If severe weather forces cancellation we work with you to reschedule.
