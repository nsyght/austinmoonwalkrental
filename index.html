What Is a Moonwalk Rental in Austin, TX?

A moonwalk is the Texas-native term for an inflatable bounce house or bouncy castle. In Austin TX and across the Greater Austin metro, locals have always called bounce houses moonwalks - a regional name that comes from the weightless, floating sensation children feel while jumping inside the inflatable chamber.

If you are searching for a moonwalk rental in Austin, a bounce house rental in Austin, or an inflatable castle rental in Austin, you are looking for the same thing. We deliver all of it across Austin, Travis County, Williamson County and the surrounding Central Texas region.

Moonwalk Rental FAQ - Austin TX

The questions Austin families ask most before booking their bounce house or moonwalk rental.

What is a moonwalk rental in Austin TX?
A moonwalk is the Texas term for an inflatable bounce house or bouncy castle. In Austin TX locals have always called bounce houses moonwalks - a regional name from the floating, weightless feeling kids get while jumping. Moonwalk, bounce house, inflatable castle and jump house all refer to the same party rental equipment.
How much does a bounce house rental cost in Austin TX?
Bounce house and moonwalk rentals in Austin TX range from $150-$350 for a standard unit, $225-$400 for a combo with slide, and $200-$450 for a water slide. All prices include delivery, setup and teardown across Austin and Travis County. Request a free quote for current pricing.
Do you deliver to Zilker Park and Austin parks?
Yes - we deliver to Zilker Park and across Austin. Austin Parks and Recreation requires a permit for inflatables at public parks. Contact Austin Parks at (512) 974-6700 before booking. We recommend securing your park permit and booking your moonwalk at the same time to lock in your date.
What Austin neighborhoods do you deliver to?
We deliver across all Austin neighborhoods - South Austin, North Austin, East Austin, West Austin, Barton Hills, Travis Heights, Tarrytown, Hyde Park, Mueller, Circle C Ranch, Steiner Ranch, Cherrywood, Bouldin Creek and more. Plus the full Greater Austin metro including Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Hutto and Round Rock.
How far in advance should I book in Austin?
For Austin summer weekends book 2-3 weeks ahead. ACL Fest weekends, UT home game weekends and holiday weekends fill especially fast. Spring and fall need 1-2 weeks notice. Same-week availability sometimes open - call (512) 555-1212 to check.
Are your bounce house rentals insured in Texas?
Yes. All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, who is fully licensed and insured in Texas. Bounce houses and moonwalks are Texas Class B Amusement Rides requiring a Texas Amusement Ride Compliance Sticker and minimum $1,000,000 liability coverage. Jump Around Party Rentals meets all Texas state requirements. See our About page for more.
What is the difference between a moonwalk and a bounce house?
They are identical equipment - different regional names. Moonwalk is the Texas term used across Austin, San Antonio, Houston and Dallas. Bounce house is the national US standard. Bouncy castle is the British term. Jump house is common in Southern US markets. All describe the same airtight inflatable structure where children jump and play.
Can bounce houses handle Austin summer heat?
Yes - with smart planning. Water slide rentals are the most popular Austin summer choice for good reason. For dry bounce houses in summer, morning setups before peak afternoon heat work best. Units are shut down if conditions exceed safe operating levels or winds hit 15-20 mph.
How long is the rental period?
Standard rentals in Austin TX cover the full event day - typically 6 to 8 hours. We deliver before your event and return after for full pickup. We arrive early so everything is inflated and inspected before your first guest arrives.
Do you deliver to Cedar Park, Georgetown and Pflugerville?
Yes - we deliver across the full Greater Austin metro including Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Hutto, Leander, Round Rock, Liberty Hill, Killeen, Belton and Temple. Delivery fees may vary by distance from our Round Rock base at 3616 Bass Loop. Contact us for exact delivery fee to your address.
Do you handle Austin ISD school carnivals?
Yes - obstacle courses, bounce houses and water slides for Austin ISD school field days and carnivals are a specialty. We can scale to fit any event size across Travis County campuses. Contact us for school event pricing and multi-unit packages.
