Austin Water Slide Setup Requirements

  • Flat open grass or pavement area - size depends on unit
  • Garden hose connection within reach of setup area
  • Standard 110V outlet within 50 feet (or generator for parks)
  • Overhead clearance - check for trees and power lines
  • Park rentals require Austin Parks permit - call (512) 974-6700

Water Slide FAQ - Austin TX

Water Slide Rental Questions

How tall are your water slides in Austin TX?
Our water slides range from 13 feet to 20+ feet tall. Smaller junior slides are ideal for younger kids and tighter yards. Our larger dual-lane and giant slides are the top choice for Austin summer birthday parties, school events and HOA gatherings. We'll recommend the right size when you contact us.
When is water slide season in Austin TX?
Water slide season in Austin runs March through October - sometimes longer. Austin summers start early and stay hot late. May through September is peak season when temperatures hit 95-105 degrees. Book 2-3 weeks ahead for summer weekends. Contact us to check availability for your date.
What do you need to set up a water slide in Austin?
A flat open area, a garden hose connection nearby and a standard 110V outlet within 50 feet. Most Austin residential lots accommodate slides up to 18 feet. For park setups a generator may be needed. We confirm all setup requirements when you book - no surprises on delivery day.
Can water slides be used at Austin parks?
Yes - we deliver to Zilker Park and other Austin parks. Austin Parks and Recreation requires a permit for inflatables. Contact Austin Parks at (512) 974-6700 before booking. Confirm electricity and water access at your chosen park. See our main FAQ for more Austin park details.
Are your water slides insured in Texas?
Yes. All rentals are provided by Jump Around Party Rentals, who is fully licensed and insured in Texas. Every water slide carries a current Texas Amusement Ride Compliance Sticker. See our About page for full details on licensing and insurance.
Do you deliver water slides to Cedar Park and Pflugerville?
Yes - we deliver across the full Greater Austin metro including Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Hutto, Leander, Round Rock and more. Contact us for exact delivery details and fees to your address.
